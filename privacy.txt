PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: March 18, 2026

EDITOR is a single-file progressive web app that runs entirely in your browser.

The app collects nothing.

• No analytics
• No tracking
• No telemetry
• No user accounts
• No data is sent to any server controlled by the developer

Local Folder mode:
Everything stays on your device. No internet is used after the app loads.

Google Drive mode:
The app only talks directly to Google’s official Drive API using your own login. 
Your files never pass through any other servers.

Local Storage:
Settings and session data are saved only in your browser (localStorage + IndexedDB).

No cookies. No third-party services.
